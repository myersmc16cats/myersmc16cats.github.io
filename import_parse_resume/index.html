Parse Resumes

Automatically parse all resumes that are added into your system. CATS creates a candidate profile from each resume and adds all keywords (from that resume) to your CATS database.

Resume Inbox

Importing resumes is as easy as using email. Forward emails with resume attachments to your CATS Resume Inbox and they are automatically parsed and added to your candidate database.
