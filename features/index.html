Features
Features

Customizable Workflow

Every company's hiring needs are unique. Nearly everything in CATS can be customized to fit your process. Create custom statuses to keep track of every step in your hiring process. Track statuses with custom colors and mappings.

Search Inside Resumes

Search for any word in the contents of every resume in your database to quickly find the candidates you’re looking for. Use Boolean search (AND, OR, NOT, etc.) to narrow down your search results. Preview candidate’s profiles and resumes without leaving your search. Keyword searches are highlighted in the results.

Detailed Reports

Get detailed reports to track your hiring. View data by user or hiring manager so you can track their effectiveness. All of your data can be exported so you can build the reports you need. Track EEO and Affirmative Action compliance.

Customizable Career Portal

Match the design of your website with a customized career portal. Customers who have branded career portals receive 5x more applicants through their portal than any other job board they post to. Use custom HTML and CSS to completely tailor the look and feel of your career portal, or get a professional custom branding created by our design team.

Import & Parse Resumes

CATS parses candidate and contact information from your resumes and creates candidate profiles automatically. Upload individual resumes or mass import with zip files or excel sheets in minutes, and CATS does the rest. Every keyword from the resume gets added into the system. Forward emails with resume attachments to your CATS Resume Inbox address and they are automatically parsed and added to your candidate database.

Keyword Search

Instantly search specific grids in CATS to find exactly what you’re looking for, fast. Use Boolean search (AND, OR, NOT, etc.) to narrow down the content in your database. Save your searches and never waste time digging around for data again.

Publish To Job Boards

Publish your jobs to the web’s most popular job boards and social networks automatically. CATS handles all the job board posting for you at no additional charge, so it’s easy! Track the source of every candidate, so you can easily know which job boards are bringing you the best candidates for your positions.

Keep Data Organized

Keep track of all the details in one place. Get detailed reports to keep you in the loop so nothing gets lost. Create lists for mailing campaigns or to quickly fill future job orders. Send mass emails to a list, add them to job orders, export them to a spreadsheet, and more. Use the Microsoft Word Add-In so you can important resumes and edit documents directly in CATS.

Chrome & Firefox Extension

Speed up your recruiting efforts with our Chrome and Firefox extensions. Automatically add candidates and resumes to CATS from popular candidate websites like LinkedIn, Indeed, Twitter, Facebook, Monster, CareerBuilder, Dice, Xing, and more!

Email Integration

You already log activities and notes in your applicant tracking system. With our email integration, you can easily store your candidate and client emails as well. Just enter your existing email credentials in a simple setup process and you’re done. Works with Gmail, Yahoo, GoDaddy, Outlook, and many more providers.

Customizable Job Applications

Customize your application with screening questionnaires. Create customer applications to pre-screen candidates that rate candidates automatically to filter out the top talent. With text fields, multiple choice, yes/no –– ask your applicants the right questions to find the right fit. Rate your candidates automatically and quickly sort the best candidates to the top of the pile.

Send Mass Emails

Search candidates by keyword and send mass emails about open positions, to notify applicants of job order updates, and more. Keep your contacts and candidates up-to-date with newsletters and opt-in messages. Easily add your candidates and contacts from MailChimp or Constant Contact mailing lists.

Hiring Manager Access [HR]

Allow your hiring managers to review positions before they’re posted to the web. Send automatic notifications for candidate reviews, approvals, and new job openings. Assign jobs to your hiring managers and control exactly what data they can and cannot see. Get feedback and notes from your hiring managers.

Submit to Clients [Recruiters]

Easily present candidates to your clients for review. Send automatic candidate submittals to your clients - integrated into your workflow. Email your clients a link to view and approve candidate submittals with one click. Track responses and status updates, automatically.

User Management

CATS was designed as a collaborative system, meaning most of your data can be seen and shared with your company. Grant users different levels of access, restrict mass export and workflow customization, and more!

Secure Data Protection

Your connection to CATS is encrypted with 256-bit SSL. Only you and the users you grant access will ever see your private data. Aside from job orders that you mark as public and post on your website, no information is ever sold or made available to any third parties. The data and information you enter in CATS is owned by you. You can create and download a backup of all of your data at any time. All of your information is stored on our servers in a 24-hour secure facility located in downtown Minneapolis. Your data is routinely backed up in multiple physical locations.
