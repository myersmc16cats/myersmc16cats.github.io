Can I import my data?

Yes! Easily import resumes with our mass import function. If you need help, we can import from virtually any other system for you. Give us a call at (952) 373-4010 or email us at support@catsone.com to discuss import options.

How secure is my data?

Your connection to CATS is encrypted with 256-bit SSL. We perform full daily backups of customer data. You can also create an on-demand backup and download all of your data.

How can I pay?

We accept all major credit cards. We do not accept checks or offer invoicing.

What about support?

All plans include phone and email support available Monday through Friday, 8:30am to 5:00pm CST.

Do I get all of the features?

Yes! All features come standard for both the Recruiter / Staffing and Direct Employer versions.

How does the free trial work?

Try CATS risk-free for 14 days. Enter your credit card any time before the trial ends to continue the service. The trial is a full version of the software, with no restrictions.
